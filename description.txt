Mod to make testing loadouts easy!

Adds a handful of commands and configs to help with testing calamity weapons

/reforge - reforges your accessories to the chosen preset
/summoner - choose from the Summoner testing loadouts!
/aftermath - Disable boss drops and aftermath on kill. Finally, the Cultist doesn't have to spawn the Celestial Pillars!
/config - Add your current gear (items only, not reforges) to the chosen class's preset list.

/melee, /ranger, /mage, /rogue - These work like /summoner, but they don't yet have any pre-built presets. You can use them with your own presets and /config, though!
